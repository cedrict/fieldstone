We find on the defelement page\footnote{\url{https://defelement.com/elements/examples/triangle-crouzeix-raviart-1.html}}
that the basis functions are given by:
\begin{eqnarray}
\bN_0(r,s)&=&2r+2s-1 \nn\\
\bN_1(r,s)&=&1-2r \nn\\
\bN_2(r,s)&=&1-2s \nn
\end{eqnarray}
We find $\bN_i(r_j,s_j)=\delta_{ij}$.
